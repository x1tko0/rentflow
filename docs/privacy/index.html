RentFlow

Privacy Policy

Overview

RentFlow is a rental management app for individual landlords. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app uses and why — in plain language.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal data on our servers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash tracking.
  • We do not share any information with third parties.
  • There are no accounts, logins, or cloud profiles in RentFlow.

Data Stored on Your Device

All your data — properties, tenants, payments, expenses — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly create a backup.

Biometric Authentication

If you enable biometric lock (fingerprint / face recognition), the app uses your device's built-in authentication system (Android BiometricPrompt). RentFlow never accesses, reads, or stores your biometric data in any form. Authentication is handled entirely by the operating system.

Google Drive Backup (Pro)

The Pro version offers an optional backup to Google Drive. When you initiate a backup, the app uploads a single encrypted database file to your personal Google Drive account — in a folder only RentFlow can access. We never read, process, or store your backup files. You can delete the backup from your Google Drive at any time.

Notifications

The app can send local push notifications (e.g., payment reminders). These are generated on-device and are not routed through any external notification service or server.

Permissions

  • USE_BIOMETRIC / USE_FINGERPRINT — biometric lock screen.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — reschedule notifications after device restart.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — display payment reminders.
  • Internet — required only for Google Drive backup (Pro).

Children's Privacy

RentFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
